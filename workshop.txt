description=[b]Better Generator Info[/b] enhances the in-game generator window with clearer, more useful details.

description=[hr][/hr]
description=[h2]Features:[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*] Shows [b]exact days and hours remaining[/b] before your generator runs out of fuel.
description=[*] Option to display remaining time in [b]real-world hours[/b] (based on sandbox day length).
description=[*] Keeps the vanilla look & feel — seamlessly extends the original [i]Generator Info Window[/i].
description=[*] Accurate calculations for Build [b]42.12+[/b] (uses the new [i]litres per hour[/i] values).
description=[*] Fuel Gauge that provides a visual representation of fuel - Can be toggled ON via Mod Options (OFF by default)
description=[*] Power Range Overlay - Display selected generator power range outline (Green when on, Red when off and (when enabled) a Unified outline of all powered areas (Powder blue).
description=[*] [b]NEW:[/b] Held Generator - Power Range Overlay
description=[*] Overlays are shown for every floor and indicate if generators are powering that floor.
description=[/list]
